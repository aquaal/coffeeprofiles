<div class="column-right">
<p>The village of Tangkul is in East Manggarai District of Flores. This part of the island is made up of small coffee farmers, who generally have less than 2 hectares of land dedicated to coffee. You see a lot of Robusta here as well as Arabica, mainly S-795 and a Catimor type that the locals call "Colombia". Altitude of these farms varies, from a low point of around 1400 meters above sea level, on up to about 1750 meters. Coffee is processed at home using hand crank depulpers, fermenting the coffee in buckets, and then either drying on racks or in makeshift drying sheds. This lot is part of an effort between ourselves and an intermediary we're working with in the region to identify, separate, and evaluate coffees from farms in the higher elevation coffee zones. This area of Tangkul is near the volcano Ranaka, a name we've used for past small-farmer blends from the region ("Poco Ranaka"). The area is rife with nutrient rich soils, and coffee farmers are able to grow using fully organic farm practices, though they are not certified at this time. </p> </div>

<div class="column-right">
<p>If you think all Indonesian coffees are earth-toned and rustic, think again. Flores Tangkul Village has perhaps more in common with balanced Central American coffees rather than some of its coffee producing neighbors. The fragrance of the dry grounds have a mild, chocolate cookie-like sweetness at City+ and roast tones are intensified with a shade more roast development. City+ roasts have a really sweet aroma with a touch of caramelized sugar. The flavors in the cup are clean and balanced, a sweetness like sugar in the raw that has elements of baked goods. As the coffee cools, a pleasant acidic impression comes through, pulling together the sweet base flavors and chocolate-y roast tone underneath. Full City+ roasts have smokey chocolate character and adds a subtle tobacco hint to the finish. Tangkul Village is versatile, middle roasts (City+) making a delicious brewed cup and Full City+ an espresso with intense chocolate roast flavors.</p> </div>
